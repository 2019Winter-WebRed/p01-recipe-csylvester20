Peanut Butter Protein Balls Recipe

This 5 ingredient protein ball that tastes like a desert is an easy and healthy on-the-go snack for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 2/3 cup Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1 cup Old Fashion Oats
  • 1/2 cup Semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup Ground Flax Seeds

Directions

  1. Combine all five of the ingredients into one bowl
  2. Stir to combine ingredients thoroughly
  3. Place in refrigerator for 15-30 minutes or overnight to so they are easier to roll
  4. Roll into 12 balls
  5. TIP: You can roll mixture into smaller pieces for more protein balls or you can can roll it into bigger pieces for less balls. Depending on the situation.
  6. ENJOY!

Prep Time: 10 minutes to mix up the mixture and no cook time required.
